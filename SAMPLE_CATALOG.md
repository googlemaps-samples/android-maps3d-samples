# 🗺️ Android Maps 3D Sample Catalog

Welcome to the definitive guide for Android Maps 3D SDK samples! To provide the best experience for your specific technology stack, we have colocated the detailed catalogs within their respective modules.

Please choose your development environment below to explore the available samples:

## 🚀 [Jetpack Compose Samples](Maps3DSamples/ComposeDemos/app/README.md)
Modern, declarative, and state-driven samples demonstrating best practices with the `maps3d-compose` library.

## 🧱 [Kotlin + Views Samples](Maps3DSamples/ApiDemos/kotlin-app/README.md)
Traditional View-based samples using Kotlin. Demonstrates a wide range of basic and advanced features.

## ☕ [Java Samples](Maps3DSamples/ApiDemos/java-app/README.md)
Traditional View-based samples using Java for developers preferring the classic language.
